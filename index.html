About Me

I am a Software Development student in the BYU-Idaho pathway, currently working through the Web and Computer Programming certificate. I enjoy building things that live on the web from small interactive scripts to full page layouts and I like the process of taking a rough idea and refining it into something that actually works well for the person using it. This course, WDD 231, is where I am sharpening my skills in responsive design, semantic HTML, and JavaScript that talks to real data.

Outside of coursework, I like tinkering with side projects, reading about design systems, and slowly turning "it works" into "it works well."
